1. Introduction

Enroute is intended to help users plan itineraries for various trips. Users can collaborate on trips with other users, making Enroute an interactive and fun platform to plan vacations.

2. Installation

To download Enroute, go to the App store on the iPhone. Search for ‘Enroute’ in the search box and select ‘GET’. Enroute 1.0 is free to download from the App store. No payment is required. Select on the application once EnRoute is downloaded on your mobile device.

3. Create Account

After launching the application, you will be required to enter the following information to login:
  • first name
  • last name
  • email
  • username
  • password

First Name: Enter your first name.

Last Name: Enter your last name.

Email: Enter your most updated email account to get regular updates for Enroute

Username: Choose a unique username. If the user name is not available/taken by a different user, the page will prompt you to re-enter a new username.

Password:Choose a unique 8 - character password. Passwords must have one upper case letter, at least one lower case letter, at least one digit. You will need to re-type your password for security.

Once these fields are filled out on the ‘Create an Account’ page, select the ‘Register’ link located near the bottom of the screen. You will be redirected to the login page. Enter your username and password to be granted access into the application.

4. Login

To login to EnRoute, you must have created an account first. Enter your username and your password in the fields. Once you have filled out the fields, select ‘Sign in’.

5. User Settings

You have the option to edit your account information on EnRoute. To access this feature, login to the application and select the hamburger menu located at the top left of the screen.

Select ‘Settings'. Here, you will have options to change your username, and update your email, or change your password.

Change Username: Enter a new username for your account. The username must be unique and must not already exist.

Update First and Last Name: Update the first and last name stored in our database. The username must be unique and must not already exist.

6. Create Trip

After you are logged in, the application will take you to your home page. Here, you can see the trips you are affiliated with. These can be trips that you have created and/or trips other users have included you in.

To create a trip, select on the add button towards the top-right of the page. You will be navigated to another page and will be prompted to fill in information about the trip. All fields are required to be filled in before you create the new instance of a trip. You will be asked to fill in a trip name, start/end date, and the location.

Trip Name*: Choose a name for your trip. Examples could be “My First Trip To Europe” or “Honeymoon”. This field is mandatory to create a unique trip within the application.

Start/end date*: Choose a start date and an end date for your trip. This field is mandatory to create a time frame for the trip.

Location: Enter a location for your trip. It could be general, such as Europe, or more precise, such as San Francisco.

Once all the fields have been filled, select ‘Create Trip’. Now, the system has created the trip for you.

7. Itinerary

Users can collaborate on an itinerary for a trip. Only the administrator of a trip may add, delete, or modify an event for the itinerary. The content of the itinerary is available as read-only for other users included in the trip.

7.1 Add a Point of Interest

Access the desired trip you would like to create an itinerary for from your home page. You can add a point of interest by selecting the plus (+) button adjacent to the label Add a Point of Interest'. You will be navigated to a new page with empty fields to fill out regarding your trip.

Title: Choose a title for your point of interest. Examples could be “Paddleboarding” or “Dinner at Il Fornario”. This field is mandatory to create a unique point of interest within the trip.

Type: Choose a type for the trip. You will be provided with the following options to classify each point of interest:

  • Activity
  • Car
  • Rental
  • Flight
  • Hotel
  • Restaurant
  • Shopping

Date: Enter the date of your planned point of interest.

Date: Enter the time of your planned point of interest.

Descrioption: Enter a short description of your point of interest

7.2 Delete a Point of Interest

Access the desired trip you would like to delete a point of interest from your profile page. Select 'Edit Itinerary'. Select the event you would like to delete from the itinerary.

7.3 Edit a Point of Interest

Access the desired trip you would like to edit a point of interest from your profile page. Select 'Trip Itinerary'. Now, select the event your would like to edit. You will be navigated to the details page of the event. Select 'Edit Event'. Here, you can change the location or time of the event.

The administrator may scroll through the itinerary and select an event they would like to modify or delete. Select ‘Edit Itinerary’ on the top of the screen. Select each event you would like to delete. Select ‘Delete’ or ‘Modify’ on the top of the screen.

8. Access the Photo Gallery for a Trip

Users will be able to share photos of the trip with other users. All users will be able to view the pictures.

Access the desired trip from your home page. Once you are on the homepage of the trip, select the photo gallery option located on the tabs on the bottom of the screen.

To add a photo, select the ‘+’ on the top-right of the screen. You will be navigated to your photo album on your mobile device. Select the desired photos you would like to be uploaded to the shared gallery of your trip. Once you are done choosing the photos, select ‘Finish’. Your photos are now on the shared gallery to view for all friends included in the trip.

9. Access the Map of a Trip

Each trip has access to a map where all the points of interest will be listed. To view the map, select on the desired trip from your home page. Select the map option from the segmented bar. Here, you will be able to view your points of interest geographically on the map.

10. Add Friends to Your Contact List

You can establish a friendship with other users who have an account with EnRoute. You can view a list of all your friends by selecting 'Friends' from the hamburger menu located at the top left of every page.

To add a friend, click the plus (+) button on the top-right corner of the 'Friends' page. You will be navigated to a page where you can type the username or full name of the user in the search box. A drop-down list will display users that match your query. Select the user you would like to add. Select plus (+) button near the searched friend's name to add the member to your friend's list. A request will be sent to the other user indicating your friendship invitation.

11. Members

Once a trip has been created, you may edit the trip by adding users. Users who are added to the trip are able to view all details about the trip. To add a friend to your trip, the user must already exist in your friends list. A friendship must have been established by adding the user to your contacts within EnRoute.

11.1 Add a Member to the Trip

Select on the desired trip you would like to add a friend to from your home page. Next, select the 'Members' tab on the bottom of the screen. Displayed will be the list of current members in the trip. To add more members to the trip, select the plus (+) button on the top-right of the screen. In the search box, type the full name or username of the friend. A drop-down list will be visible with the search results of your query. Select the plus (+) near your friend's name. Your friend is now included in the trip and will be able to view all details.
