1. Introduction

Enroute is intended to help users plan itineraries for various trips. Users can collaborate on trips with other users, making Enroute an interactive and fun platform to plan vacations.

3. Create Account

After launching the application, you will be required to enter the following information to login:
  • email
  • username
  • password
  • first name
  • last name
  • phone number

Email: Enter your most updated email account to get regular updates for Enroute

Username: Choose a unique username. If the user name is not available/taken by a different user, the page will prompt you to re-enter a new username.

Password:Choose a unique 8 - character password. You will need to retype your password for security.

First Name: Enter your first name.

Last Name: Enter your last name.

Phone Number: Enter a valid phone number. If the phone number is not valid, the page will prompt you to re-enter your correct phone number.

Once these fields are filled out on the â€˜Create an Accountâ€™ page, select the â€˜Create Accountâ€™ link located near the bottom of the screen. You will be redirected to the login page. Enter your username and password to be granted access into the application.

5. User Settings

You have the option to edit your account information on EnRoute. To access this feature, login to the application and navigate to your profile page.

Select â€˜User Settingsâ€™. Here, you will have options to change your username, change your profile picture, update your privacy settings, and update your notification settings.

Change Username: Enter a new username for your account. The username must be unique and must not already exist.

Change Profile Picture: You will be navigated to your photo album on your mobile device. Choose a desired photo for your profile.

Update Privacy Settings: You can change how you would like others to view your profile. Private mode only makes your name and username viewable to the public. Public mode allows all users of EnRoute to view your trips and itineraries.

Update Notification Settings: You can update whether you would like notifications sent to your mobile device from EnRoute.

7. Itinerary

Users can collaborate on an itinerary for a trip. Only the administrator of a trip may add, delete, or modify an event for the itinerary. The content of the itinerary is available as read-only for other users included in the trip.

Add a Point of Interest

Access the desired trip you would like to create an itinerary for from your profile page. Select â€˜Trip Itineraryâ€™. If you do not have an itinerary created, the page will prompt you to create one by adding at least one event. Select a point of interest for the event. Select a time for the event.

Delete a Point of Interest

Access the desired trip you would like to delete a point of interest from your profile page. Select 'Edit Itinerary'. Select the event you would like to delete from the itinerary.

Edit a Point of Interest

Access the desired trip you would like to edit a point of interest from your profile page. Select 'Trip Itinerary'. Now, select the event your would like to edit. You will be navigated to the details page of the event. Select 'Edit Event'. Here, you can change the location or time of the event.

The administrator may scroll through the itinerary and select an event they would like to modify or delete. Select â€˜Edit Itineraryâ€™ on the top of the screen. Select each event you would like to delete. Select â€˜Deleteâ€™ or â€˜Modifyâ€™ on the top of the screen.

If a trip has been established, users of the same trip may chat with one another. You may start individual chats with one user or you may start a group chat with multiple users.

Access the desired trip from your profile page. On the homepage of the trip, select â€˜Chatâ€™. You will be navigated to the chat feature of the application. Users will be able to share photos of the trip with other users. All users will be able to view the pictures. Only the user who uploaded the photo to the gallery will be able to remove the photo.

Access the desired trip from your profile page. Once you are on the homepage of the trip, select the photo gallery option located on the tabs on the bottom of the screen.

To add a photo, select the â€˜+â€™ on the top-right of the screen. You will be navigated to your photo album on your mobile device. 10. Access the Map of a Trip

Each trip has access to a map where all the points of interest will be listed. To view the map, select on the desired trip from your profile page. Select the map option on the bottom of the screen. Here, you will be able to view your points of interest geographically on the map.

Once a trip has been created, you may edit the trip by adding users. Users who are added to the trip are able to view all details about the trip. To add a friend to your trip, the user must already exist in your friends list. A friendship must have been established by adding the user to your contacts within EnRoute.

Select on the desired trip you would like to add a friend to from your profile page. You will be navigated to the trip details page. Select on â€˜Add Friendâ€™.
